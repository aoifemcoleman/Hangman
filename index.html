    How to Play

    • The blank spaces above each represent a missing letter in the word.
    • Using the keyboard, you must guess letters to fill each blank space.
    • As you guess correctly, the letters will populate in their relevant space within the word.
    • The more letters you guess, the closer you will be to guessing the full word.
    • Be careful though - as you guess incorrectly, you will lose lives and the hangman will start to appear!
